Field Trips of 2016

Film enthusiasts will recognise the oddly shapped rocks in the Lord Of The Rings at Castle Hill.

Quick montage of the two field trips during 2016

Castle Hill

Advanced Field Mapping

This field trip aimed to broaden the geological experience at the advanced level. With being able to interoperate more complex geological features such as anti-synclines and faults which were a welcomed challenge. The second half focused on geomorphology and surface features interpretations.


Southland Geology

Advanced Field Techniques


Field trips are back again in 2016, this will be the furthest south that most of us have been. The coursed aimed to integrate different types of geologic data to interpret a geologic history of a metamorphic basement geology, structural geology, basin analysis and sedimentary depositional environments, as well as quarries and coal mines.
