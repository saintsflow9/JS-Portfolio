Leading

During my time at university I made a conscious effort to turn my fears into strengths.
One of my greatest fears was public speaking and being a leader.
For this reason I signed up to be a lab and field trip demonstrator.

The lab demonstrator's role is one that explains and demonstrates lecture material in class. There is typically one demonstrator (teacher) per 15 students. I was on charge of two streams one class was small and the other had ~32 students. Another responsibility is marking reports and exams. This must be done with great care as your feedback is used to help the student in areas that he/she may lack. I was a lab demonstrator for two courses and two field trips during my time at university.

I found teaching to be a rewarding experience. While initially dificult and at times did test my patience, I always felt it was my responsibility to be well prepared to deliver the lesson as effectively as possible.
